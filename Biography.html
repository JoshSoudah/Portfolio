Joshua Soudah

Graphic Design Student 

My name is Joshua Soudah. I was born in San Antonio, Tx on November 10, 2000. Although I was born in San Antonio, I grew up about 45 min south-west in a small subdivision named Lake Wood Estates in Pipe Creek, Tx. My father was a ship captain for oil research while my mother was a hair stylist. My father's love of the water brought our family to Panama City, Fl in 2015, transitioning us to the Florida beaches rather than Texas hills. While in high schoo, I found my passion in Graphic design, and ever since, I have been pursuing my Bachelors degree with the desire of attaining a position in UX Design.
