Artist Statement

Welcome to my collage project. The goal for this project was to create three highly creative and imaginative compositions that each had different relationships with a group of descriptive words relating to me. The composition on the left relates to my body. The composition in the middle relates to my mind, and the composition on the right relates to my spirit. Each element was thoughtfully placed in its location with the intent of creating unity.
